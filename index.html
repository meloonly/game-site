About Kodiqi Idle Breakout

Kodiqi Idle Breakout is an innovative idle game that reimagines the classic brick-breaker concept. Developed with modern gaming principles in mind, it offers a perfect blend of active gameplay and passive progression that keeps players engaged for hours.

Unlike traditional brick breakers where you actively control the paddle, in Kodiqi Idle Breakout, you set up your strategy and watch as the game plays itself. Earn in-game currency to upgrade your balls, buy special abilities, and break through increasingly challenging levels.

Whether you have a few minutes to spare or want to optimize your setup for maximum efficiency, Kodiqi Idle Breakout offers endless entertainment that fits your schedule.

How to Play

Getting Started

  1. Launch the game by clicking the "Play Now" button above.
  2. Watch as your initial balls automatically bounce and break bricks.
  3. Collect the currency that drops from broken bricks.
  4. Use your earnings to purchase upgrades in the shop.

Advanced Strategies

  • Focus on upgrading ball damage first to progress through early levels quickly.
  • Balance between increasing the number of balls and improving their individual power.
  • Unlock special abilities that complement your playstyle.
  • Consider prestige options when your progress begins to slow down.

Tips & Tricks

  • The game continues to progress even when you're not actively playing.
  • Check back regularly to collect offline earnings and apply new upgrades.
  • Experiment with different combinations of balls and abilities to find optimal setups.
  • Join the Kodiqi community to share strategies and discoveries with other players.
